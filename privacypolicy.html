Privacy Policy for BW Tutorial Browser Extension Last updated: 09/11/2024 This Privacy Policy explains how the BW Tutorial Browser Extension will collect, use, and safeguard your information. 1. Information Collected We collect the following data to enhance your experience with the extension: User Activity: We track user interactions such as clicks, mouse positions, scrolling, and keystrokes within the context of the extension’s tutorials and guidance features. Website Content: The extension may access text, images, sounds, videos, or hyperlinks on web pages to provide context-specific guidance, such as triggering pop-ups based on user interactions with specific elements on a page. 2. How We Use Collected Information The data collected is used solely to provide and improve the functionality of the extension. Specifically, we use this data to: Deliver relevant tutorial content and guidance based on user actions. Monitor usage to ensure the extension is functioning as intended. Improve future versions of the extension based on user interaction data. 3. Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any personal or sensitive information to outside parties. All data collected by the extension remains on your local device and is only used for the purposes described above. 4. Security We prioritize the security of your information. The data we collect is only used locally within the extension and is not transmitted to any external servers. We employ industry-standard security measures to ensure the protection of your data. 5. Your Choices You have control over the data collection by the extension. If you wish to disable certain features or uninstall the extension, you can do so at any time via your browser’s extension management settings. 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last updated" date. 7.
